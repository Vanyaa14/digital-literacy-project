Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or click on suspicious links
- Always verify payment requests before accepting them
- Use only official apps for online transactions
- Enable two-factor authentication for extra security
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
- Do not trust unknown calls claiming to be from banks or support teams
- Use strong passwords for your accounts
- Immediately report fraud on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
