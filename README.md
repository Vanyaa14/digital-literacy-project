Hi, I’m **Vanya Singh**, a first-year B.Tech student in **Computer Science and Engineering (AI & ML)** at **VIT Bhopal University**.

## 📌 Project Overview  
This project is based on the role of a *Student Digital Ambassador*. The aim was to understand how students can effectively use digital tools, communicate professionally, and stay safe in the online world.  

Through this project, I completed five tasks that helped me gain practical experience with digital platforms, improve communication skills, and develop awareness about cyber safety.

## 📝 Task Summaries  

### 🔹 Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic  
Created an infographic using Canva to explain digital literacy, useful tools, safe internet practices, and professional online behavior in a simple and visual way.  

---

### 🔹 Task 2: Digital Portfolio  
Built my digital presence by creating profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This helped me understand how to present myself professionally online.  

---

### 🔹 Task 3: Platforms Exploration  
Practiced coding on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy. This task helped me explore tools used for learning, collaboration, and data collection.  

---

### 🔹 Task 4: Email Etiquette  
Wrote professional emails and created a social media checklist. This task improved my understanding of formal communication and responsible online behavior.  

---

### 🔹 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness  
Studied UPI/online payment fraud and created a prevention checklist. This helped me understand common cyber threats and how to stay safe online.
