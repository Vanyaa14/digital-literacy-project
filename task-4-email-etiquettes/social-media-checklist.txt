Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s:
- Keep your profile professional and clean
- Think before posting anything online
- Use privacy settings wisely
- Share useful and positive content
- Respect others’ opinions and maintain good behavior

Don’ts:
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
- Do not engage in online arguments or negativity
- Avoid accepting requests from unknown people
- Do not spread fake or unverified information
